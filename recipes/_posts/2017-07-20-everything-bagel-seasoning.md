I love seeing how much play TJ’s “Everything But The Bagel” seasoning gets in the keto community. I wanna tell ya, it’s super easy to make at home and you can get a LOT more bang for your buck making it yourself, especially if you have a shop where you can buy spices in bulk.

Jazz up even MORE of your low carb snacks without worrying that the store is going to a) run out or b) discontinue. And btw - this makes Fathead bagels SO GOOD. Making some for a client right now!

Recipe type: sauce, dip, condiment

Cuisine: European, American, Californian, Vegetarian

Serves: 1/2 cup

&nbsp;

### INGREDIENTS

&nbsp;

* 2 Tablespoons poppy seeds
* 1 heaping Tablespoon garlic granules
* 1 heaping Tablespoon dehydrated onion flakes
* 1 Tablespoon white sesame seeds
* 1 Tablespoon black sesame seeds
* 1/2 Tablespoon caraway seeds (optional)
* Big pinch coarse salt (optional)

&nbsp;

### INSTRUCTIONS

&nbsp;

1. Mix ‘em all up in a bowl and store in an airtight container. Sprinkle liberally!!
